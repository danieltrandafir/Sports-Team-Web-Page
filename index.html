About us

A group of friends wanting to get together and keep active.

How we do it?

The football pitch is booked every Sunday at 9am for two hours.
We play a 7 a side game with a 10 min break.

Why we love it?

Life is busy wheather we want it or not, this is a moment to detach from everything and have some stress free fun with the boys.

Where we do it?

We play at the Braunstone Grove Community Hub, Cort Crescent, Leicester
